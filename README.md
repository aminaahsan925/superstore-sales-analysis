## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs a complete end-to-end data analysis on a retail superstore's
sales data. The goal is to uncover business insights that help management make
smarter decisions about products, regions, discounts, and customer segments.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

- Which product category generates the most revenue?
- Which region is most and least profitable?
- How do monthly sales trend throughout the year?
- Does giving higher discounts actually cause profit loss?
- Which customer segment contributes the most to total sales?

---

---

## 📊 Key Findings

- **Technology** is the highest revenue category at $11,750
- **West region** leads in total profit
- **High discounts (30%+) directly cause profit losses** — shown in scatter analysis
- **Corporate customers** drive the largest share of sales
- Sales peak in **August and November**

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. **Reduce discounts above 20%** — data shows they cause direct losses
2. **Invest more in Technology category** — highest profit margins
3. **Focus marketing on Corporate segment** — highest sales volume
4. **Investigate South region** — showing lowest profit performance
